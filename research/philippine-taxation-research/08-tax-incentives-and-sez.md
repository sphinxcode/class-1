# Philippine Tax Incentives and Special Economic Zones

## Executive Summary

The Philippines has implemented a robust tax incentive framework designed to attract both foreign and domestic investment. The cornerstone legislation is the **Corporate Recovery and Tax Incentives for Enterprises (CREATE) Act (RA 11534)**, signed on March 26, 2021, and subsequently enhanced by the **CREATE MORE Act (RA 12066)**, signed on November 11, 2024.

---

## 1. CREATE Law Incentive Tiers and Durations

### Strategic Investment Priority Plan (SIPP) Tier Structure

| Tier | Focus Areas | Examples |
|------|-------------|----------|
| **Tier I** | Basic needs, sustainability-driven industries | Healthcare, disaster risk reduction, agriculture, energy |
| **Tier II** | Import-substituting activities, strategic sectors | Green ecosystems, defense-related activities |
| **Tier III** | High-technology and innovation | R&D, advanced manufacturing, AI, data centers |

### Income Tax Holiday (ITH) Durations

**Under CREATE Act:**

| Enterprise Type | ITH Duration | Post-ITH Incentive | Total Period |
|-----------------|--------------|--------------------|--------------|
| Export Enterprise | 4-7 years | 10 years SCIT (5%) OR EDR | 14-17 years |
| Domestic Market Enterprise | 4-7 years | 5 years EDR | 9-12 years |

**Under CREATE MORE Act (Enhanced):**

| Enterprise Type | ITH Duration | Post-ITH Incentive | Total Period |
|-----------------|--------------|--------------------|--------------|
| Export Enterprise | 4-7 years | 20 years SCIT OR EDR | 24-27 years |
| Domestic Market Enterprise | 4-7 years | 20 years EDR | 24-27 years |

**Maximum Limits:**
- ITH shall not exceed 8 years
- Total period of incentive availment shall not exceed 40 years

## 2. PEZA Incentives

### Income Tax Holiday (ITH)

| Project Classification | ITH Duration |
|------------------------|--------------|
| Export-oriented Enterprises | 4-7 years |
| Domestic Market Enterprises | 4-7 years |
| Less Developed Areas | Up to 6 years |

### Special Corporate Income Tax (SCIT)

- **Rate:** 5% on gross income earned (GIE)
- **Duration:** 10 years after ITH period
- **Availability:** Export enterprises only
- **Scope:** In lieu of ALL national and local taxes

### Additional PEZA Fiscal Incentives

| Incentive | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Duty-Free Importation** | Capital equipment, raw materials, spare parts |
| **VAT Exemption** | Importation for registered projects |
| **VAT Zero-Rating** | Local purchases for registered activity |
| **Domestic Sales Allowance** | Up to 30% of total sales for export enterprises |
| **Wharfage Dues Exemption** | 10 years from registration |

## 3. BOI Registered Enterprise Benefits

### Income Tax Holiday Periods

| Project Type | ITH Duration |
|--------------|--------------|
| New projects - Pioneer status | 6 years |
| New projects - Non-pioneer status | 4 years |
| Expansion projects | 3 years |
| Projects in Less Developed Areas | 6 years |
| Modernization projects | 3 years |

### Enhanced Deductions Regime (EDR)

| Deduction Type | Rate |
|----------------|------|
| Research and Development | 100% |
| Training Expenses | 100% |
| Power Expenses | 100% |
| Depreciation - Buildings | 10% |
| Depreciation - Machinery/Equipment | 20% |
| Labor Expenses | 50% |
| Domestic Input Expenses | 50% |
| Exhibitions, Trade Fairs | 50% |
| Reinvestment Allowance (Manufacturing) | 50% |

### Corporate Income Tax Rate Under EDR

- **CREATE Act:** 25% CIT rate
- **CREATE MORE Act:** Reduced to 20% CIT rate

### Non-Fiscal Incentives

- Employment of foreign nationals (5 years, extendible)
- Guaranteed repatriation of foreign investments
- Importation of consigned equipment (unlimited period)

## 4. Freeport Zone Incentives

### Clark Freeport and Special Economic Zone

| Incentive | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Income Tax Holiday | 4-7 years |
| Special Corporate Income Tax | 5% on gross income for 10 years |
| Duty Exemption | Capital equipment, raw materials, spare parts |
| Foreign Ownership | Up to 100% allowed |

### Subic Special Economic and Freeport Zone

| Incentive | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Tax Rate | 5% of gross income in lieu of all other taxes |
| Local Sales Limit | Not exceed 30% |
| Duty/Tax Exemption | Imported raw materials, capital equipment |
| Local Tax Exemption | No VAT, excise, property tax, franchise tax |

### Freeport Area of Bataan (AFAB)

| Incentive | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Income Tax Holiday | 4-8 years (extendible) |
| Tax Regime | CREATE Act incentives apply |

## 5. Strategic Investment Priority Plan (SIPP)

### Current Framework (2022-2025)

**Tier I Activities:**
- Agriculture, fisheries, forestry
- Manufacturing (essential products)
- Healthcare and pharmaceuticals
- Disaster risk reduction
- Environment/climate change projects
- Mass housing
- Energy

**Tier II Activities:**
- Goods and services not locally produced
- Import-substituting activities
- Defense-related activities
- Industrial value chain gaps
- Food security activities

**Tier III Activities:**
- Research and development
- Advanced manufacturing
- Artificial intelligence
- Cybersecurity
- Data center facilities
- Innovation support facilities
- IT-BPM services

## 6. Incentives for Exporters

### VAT Benefits

| Benefit | Requirement |
|---------|-------------|
| VAT Zero-Rating | Local purchases for registered activity |
| VAT Exemption | Importations for registered project |
| Export Sales Threshold | At least 70% of total annual production |

### CREATE MORE Act Enhancements

- Expanded VAT zero-rating to ALL sales to Export-Oriented Enterprises (EOEs)
- Exempted EOE importations from VAT
- Simplified documentation (VAT Zero-Rating Certificate only)

### Disqualification Consequences

Failure to meet the 70% export sales threshold results in:
- Disqualification from duty exemption
- Loss of VAT zero-rating on local purchases

## 7. Sunset Provisions for Old Incentives

### Transition Period for Pre-CREATE Registered Enterprises

| Current Status | Transition Period |
|----------------|-------------------|
| ITH only granted before CREATE | Continue ITH for remaining period |
| ITH + entitled to 5% GIE | Continue ITH, then 5% GIE for 10 years |
| Currently availing 5% GIE | Continue for 10 years |

### Sunset Period Based on GIE Duration

| GIE Incentive Enjoyed | Sunset Period |
|-----------------------|---------------|
| 10 years or more | 4 years |
| 5-10 years | 5 years |
| Less than 5 years | 7 years |
| Export, employing 10,000+ workers | 9 years |

### CREATE MORE Update

Pre-CREATE RBEs have until **December 31, 2034** to fully transition.

## 8. Application and Compliance Requirements

### PEZA Registration

**Eligibility:**
- Must be located within PEZA-accredited SEZ or IT park
- Export-oriented: at least 70% of production/services for export

**Required Documents:**
- SEC/DTI registration
- Company profile and organizational structure
- Feasibility study
- Environmental compliance certificates

### BOI Registration

**Eligibility:**
- Business activity aligns with SIPP
- Foreign companies: Export at least 70% OR fall within preferred investment areas

**Requirements:**
- BOI Application Form with supporting documents
- Project Report containing SIPP-listed activities

### FIRB Approval Thresholds

| Investment Capital | Approval Authority |
|-------------------|-------------------|
| PHP 15 billion and below | Investment Promotion Agencies (IPAs) |
| More than PHP 15 billion | FIRB (upon IPA recommendation) |

### Compliance and Reporting Requirements

**Annual Requirements:**
- SEC General Information Sheet (GIS)
- Audited Financial Statements
- Annual Income Tax Returns
- PEZA/BOI performance reports
- Summary of export sales performance
- Employment reports

---

## Regulatory Bodies and Oversight

### Fiscal Incentives Review Board (FIRB)

**Key Functions:**
1. Policy making and oversight on tax incentive administration
2. Approve/disapprove grants of tax incentives (projects >PHP 15B)
3. Cancel, suspend, or withdraw tax incentives
4. Conduct inquiries and investigations

### Investment Promotion Agencies (IPAs)

- **PEZA** - Philippine Economic Zone Authority
- **BOI** - Board of Investments
- **CDC** - Clark Development Corporation
- **SBMA** - Subic Bay Metropolitan Authority
- **AFAB** - Authority of the Freeport Area of Bataan
