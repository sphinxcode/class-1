# Philippine Special Taxes and Fees

## Executive Summary

This report provides a comprehensive analysis of special taxes, fees, and mandatory contributions in the Philippines, covering travel tax, social contributions (PhilHealth, SSS, GSIS, Pag-IBIG), motor vehicle user's charge, environmental compliance fees, mining taxes, gaming taxes, telecommunications franchise taxes, and energy sector taxes.

---

## 1. Travel Tax

### Legal Basis
- **Presidential Decree No. 1183** (August 28, 1977)
- **Republic Act No. 9593** (Tourism Act of 2009)

### Current Rates (2026)

| Category | First Class | Economy Class |
|----------|-------------|---------------|
| Full Travel Tax | PHP 2,700 | PHP 1,620 |
| Reduced Travel Tax | PHP 1,350 | PHP 810 |
| Privileged Reduced Rate (OFW dependents) | PHP 400 | PHP 300 |

### Exemptions
- Infants (2 years old and below)
- Overseas Filipino Workers (OFWs)
- Students with government scholarships
- Foreign tourists (less than one year)
- Diplomats

### Administering Agency
Tourism Infrastructure and Enterprise Zone Authority (TIEZA)

## 2. Philippine Health Insurance Corporation (PhilHealth) Contributions

### Legal Basis
- **Republic Act No. 11223** (Universal Health Care Act)

### Current Rates (2026)

| Parameter | Rate/Amount |
|-----------|-------------|
| Premium Rate | 5% of monthly basic salary |
| Salary Floor | PHP 10,000 |
| Salary Ceiling | PHP 100,000 |
| Minimum Monthly Contribution | PHP 500 |
| Maximum Monthly Contribution | PHP 5,000 |

### Cost Sharing
- **Employee Share**: 2.5%
- **Employer Share**: 2.5%

## 3. Social Security System (SSS) Contributions

### Legal Basis
- **Republic Act No. 11199** (Social Security Act of 2018)

### Current Rates (2026)

| Parameter | Rate/Amount |
|-----------|-------------|
| Total Contribution Rate | 15% of Monthly Salary Credit |
| Employee Share | 5% |
| Employer Share | 10% |
| Minimum MSC | PHP 5,000 |
| Maximum MSC | PHP 35,000 |

### Mandatory Provident Fund (MPF)
- Applies to members with MSC of PHP 20,000 or higher
- Contributions for MSC in excess of PHP 20,000 up to PHP 35,000

---

## 4. Government Service Insurance System (GSIS) Contributions

### Legal Basis
- **Republic Act No. 8291** (GSIS Act of 1997)

### Current Rates

| Parameter | Rate |
|-----------|------|
| Total Premium Rate | 21% of monthly compensation |
| Personal Share (Employee) | 9% |
| Government Share (Employer) | 12% |

### Coverage
Compulsory for all government employees including uniformed personnel (AFP, PNP, BFP, BJMP).

## 5. Pag-IBIG Fund (HDMF) Contributions

### Legal Basis
- **Republic Act No. 9679** (Home Development Mutual Fund Law of 2009)
- **HDMF Circular No. 460** (January 15, 2024)

### Current Rates (Effective February 2024)

| Monthly Compensation | Employee Share | Employer Share |
|---------------------|----------------|----------------|
| PHP 1,500 and below | 1% | 2% |
| Over PHP 1,500 | 2% | 2% |

### Maximum Fund Salary (MFS)
- Increased from PHP 5,000 to PHP 10,000
- Maximum contribution: PHP 200 each (employee and employer)
- Total maximum monthly: PHP 400

## 6. Motor Vehicle User's Charge (MVUC)

### Legal Basis
- **Republic Act No. 8794** (June 27, 2000)
- **Republic Act No. 11239**

### Current Rates

| Vehicle Type | MVUC Rate |
|--------------|-----------|
| Motorcycle (without sidecar) | PHP 240 |
| Motorcycle (with sidecar) | PHP 300 |
| Light passenger cars (up to 1,600 kg) | PHP 1,600 |
| Medium passenger cars (1,601-2,300 kg) | PHP 3,600 |
| Heavy passenger cars (2,301 kg and above) | PHP 8,000 |
| Utility vehicles (up to 2,700 kg) | PHP 2,000 |

### Electric Vehicle Discounts
- Electric Vehicles: 30% discount
- Hybrid Electric Vehicles: 15% discount

## 8. Mining Taxes and Royalties

### Legal Basis
- **Republic Act No. 12253** (Enhanced Mining Fiscal Regime) - September 2025
- **Republic Act No. 10963** (TRAIN Act)

### Current Tax Structure

| Tax Type | Rate |
|----------|------|
| Excise Tax on Mineral Products | 4% |
| Royalty (Mineral Reservations) | 5% |
| Royalty (Outside Reservations) | 1-5% (margin-based) |
| Minimum Royalty | 0.1% of gross output |
| Windfall Profits Tax | 1-10% (margin-based) |
| Corporate Income Tax | 25% |

### Indigenous Peoples Royalty
Minimum 1% of gross output payable to concerned indigenous cultural communities.

### Revenue Sharing with LGUs
LGUs entitled to up to 40% of gross national collections from mining taxes.

## 9. Gaming Taxes (PAGCOR, Casinos)

### Legal Basis
- **Presidential Decree No. 1869** (PAGCOR Charter)
- **Revenue Memorandum Circular No. 132-2024**

### PAGCOR Franchise Tax

| Category | Tax Rate |
|----------|----------|
| Gaming Operations Income | 5% franchise tax (in lieu of all taxes) |
| Non-Gaming Operations | Regular CIT + VAT |

### Tax on Player Winnings

| Winnings Amount | Tax Treatment |
|-----------------|---------------|
| Over PHP 10,000 | 20% final withholding tax |
| PHP 10,000 or less | Included in gross income |

---

## 10. Telecommunications Franchise Taxes

### Legal Basis
- Individual Congressional Franchises
- **Republic Act No. 7160** (Local Government Code)

### Franchise Tax Rates

| Company | Rate |
|---------|------|
| Standard Rate | 3% of gross receipts |
| Globe Telecom | 1.5% of gross receipts |
| Smart Communications | 3% of gross receipts |

### Local Government Franchise Tax
- Rate: Not exceeding 50% of 1% (0.5%) of gross annual receipts

## 11. Energy Sector Taxes

### Legal Basis
- **Republic Act No. 9136** (EPIRA)
- **Republic Act No. 9337** - VAT imposition
- **Republic Act No. 10963** (TRAIN Law)

### Electricity VAT Structure

| Sector | Tax Treatment |
|--------|---------------|
| Power Generation | Zero-rated |
| Transmission | Subject to VAT |
| Distribution | Subject to VAT |
| Renewable Energy | Zero-rated |

### Excise Taxes on Petroleum Products (TRAIN Law)

| Product | Current Rate |
|---------|-------------|
| Gasoline | PHP 10 per liter |
| Diesel | PHP 6 per liter |
| Kerosene | PHP 5 per liter |
| LPG | PHP 3 per kg |
