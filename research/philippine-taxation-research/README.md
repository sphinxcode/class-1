## Key Tax Rates Summary (2026)

### Income Tax

| Category | Rate |
|----------|------|
| Individual (Tax-free threshold) | PHP 250,000 |
| Individual (Maximum rate) | 35% |
| Corporate (Standard) | 25% |
| Corporate (MSME) | 20% |

### Consumption Taxes

| Tax Type | Rate |
|----------|------|
| VAT | 12% |
| Non-VAT Percentage Tax | 3% |
| Stock Transaction Tax | 0.1% |

### Transfer Taxes

| Tax Type | Rate |
|----------|------|
| Estate Tax | 6% flat |
| Donor's Tax | 6% flat |
| DST on Real Property | 1.5% |

### Social Contributions

| Contribution | Rate |
|--------------|------|
| PhilHealth | 5% (2.5% each) |
| SSS | 15% (5% EE, 10% ER) |
| GSIS | 21% (9% EE, 12% ER) |
| Pag-IBIG | 2% each (max PHP 200) |

## Filing Deadlines Quick Reference

### Annual Returns

| Return | Deadline |
|--------|----------|
| Individual/Corporate ITR | April 15 |
| Form 2316 (Employee Certificates) | January 31 |
| Form 1604-C (Compensation) | January 31 |
| Form 1604-E (EWT) | March 1 |

### Monthly/Quarterly Returns

| Return | Deadline |
|--------|----------|
| Monthly Withholding | 10th of following month |
| Quarterly VAT | 25th day after quarter end |
| Quarterly ITR | 15th of month after quarter |
