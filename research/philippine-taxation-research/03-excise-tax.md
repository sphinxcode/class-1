# Philippine Excise Tax Laws (2024-2026)

## Executive Summary

Philippine excise taxes are governed primarily by the Tax Reform for Acceleration and Inclusion (TRAIN) Law (Republic Act No. 10963), along with subsequent amendments including RA 11346 (tobacco reform), RA 11467 (alcohol and e-cigarettes), and RA 12253 (mining fiscal reform). In 2024, the government collected P303 billion in excise taxes.

---

## 1. Products Subject to Excise Tax

### A. Alcohol Products
- Distilled spirits (whisky, brandy, rum, gin, vodka, tequila)
- Wines (sparkling and still)
- Fermented liquors (beer, ale, porter, stout)

### B. Tobacco Products
- Cigarettes (machine-packed and hand-rolled)
- Heated tobacco products (HTPs)
- Vapor/e-cigarette products (salt nicotine and classic nicotine)

### C. Petroleum Products
- Gasoline
- Diesel
- Kerosene
- Liquefied Petroleum Gas (LPG)
- Bunker fuel oil

### D. Automobiles
- Passenger vehicles based on manufacturer's selling price
- Pickup trucks (effective July 1, 2025)
- Exemptions for electric vehicles; 50% rate for hybrids

### E. Sweetened Beverages
- Beverages using caloric sweeteners
- Beverages using non-caloric sweeteners
- Beverages using high fructose corn syrup (HFCS)

### F. Minerals
- Metallic minerals (copper, gold, chromite, etc.)
- Non-metallic minerals
- Quarry resources
- Coal and coke

### G. Cosmetic Procedures
- Invasive cosmetic surgeries and procedures

| Year | Excise Tax Rate (per Metric Ton) |
|------|----------------------------------|
| 2020-2026 | P150.00 |

#### New Mining Law (RA 12253 - Effective September 2025)
- Retains 4% excise tax on metallic minerals
- Introduces windfall profits tax (1%-10% based on profit margins exceeding 30%)
- 5% royalty for mines in mineral reservations

### G. COSMETIC PROCEDURES

| Procedure Type | Excise Tax Rate |
|----------------|-----------------|
| Invasive cosmetic procedures | 5% of gross receipts (net of excise tax and VAT) |

**Covered Procedures Include:**
- Liposuction
- Mammoplasty/breast lift
- Facelift and neck lift
- Hair restoration/transplantation
- Rhinoplasty
- Abdominoplasty (tummy tuck)

**Exemptions:**
- Procedures to ameliorate congenital/developmental defects
- Treatment of injuries from accidents or trauma
- Treatment of disfiguring disease, tumor, virus, or infection
- Non-invasive procedures (facials, peeling, laser treatments)

## 4. Sin Tax Reform Provisions

### RA 10351 (Sin Tax Reform Act of 2012)
- Simplified cigarette tax from 4-tier to unitary system by 2017
- 4% annual increase to keep pace with inflation
- Earmarked revenues for Universal Health Care

### RA 11346 (Tobacco Tax Reform of 2019)
- Increased cigarette tax from P35 to P45 per pack in 2020
- 5% annual increases from 2024 onwards
- Imposed new taxes on e-cigarettes and heated tobacco products

### RA 11467 (Alcohol and E-Cigarette Tax of 2020)
- Increased and simplified alcohol excise tax structure
- Established 6% annual increase for all alcohol products from 2025
